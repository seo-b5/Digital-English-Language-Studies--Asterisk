1. Introduction

Studying Latin and Greek plural forms in English is important because these ancient languages are the foundation of Western academic writing. Over 60% of English words come from Latin and Greek. knowing their plural rules helps us communicate more clearly in professional jobs. In this report, our team looks at which nouns are changing to regular English plurals and which ones still use their old forms.
